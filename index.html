About Us

The Interfraternity Council (IFC) is the governing body for male social fraternities at the University of Delaware. It is responsible for maintaining the community amongst these chapters, programming, and enforement of rules and regulations. Together, the Interfraternity Council and its chapters have worked very hard to create a Greek community we can be proud of.

Learn More

Our Community

Our brothers represent some of the best gentlemen on campus, serving as New Student Orientation Leaders, members of the Student Government Association, Resident Assistants, and members of a number of other Registered Student Organizations. Many of our prothers are also involved with a number of athletic teams and clubs.

Explore our Chapters

Our Events

As social chapters, fraternities hold several social events throughout each semester. They hold mixers with sororities and participate in weeklong events such as Homecoming Week and Greek Week. Giving back to our communit is also important. Every Greek organization on this campus has a larger cause they support, such as the B+ Foundation, Children's Miracle Network, The Ronald McDonald House, the Make a Wish Foundation, and more.

Go Greek

There are many benefits of joining an Interfraternity Council Chapter. Our brothers share special bonds and develop life-long friendships. Joining a fraternity provides you with an extensive suppose system, and allows you to become a part of something bigger than yourself. Each fraternity is unique and diverse in its own way, and we can assure you that at UD there is a fraternity just for you.
