How to rush

Fraternity Rush occurs during the first month of every semester, lasting for around 2 weeks. Fraternities host rush events at local restaurants and other off-campus locations as an opportunity for you to meet and learn about their organization. The Interfraternity Council releases rush dates and locations for each Fraternity at the beginning of the semester. Our most recently updated rush schedule is provided below.

About Us

The Interfraternity Council (IFC) is the governing body for male social fraternities at the University of Delaware. It is responsible for maintaining the community amongst these chapters, programming, and enforement of rules and regulations. Together, the Interfraternity Council and its chapters have worked very hard to create a Greek community we can be proud of.
